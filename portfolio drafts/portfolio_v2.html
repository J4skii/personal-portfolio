How I Work

I'm a technical analyst and full-stack developer at Praeto Risk & Insurance Management Solutions. I spend time between discovering what businesses actually need and building what works.

Most developers start with code. Most business analysts stop at specs. I do both—which means I catch gaps before they become production incidents.

My superpower: Taking a messy problem, translating it clearly for technical teams, then implementing it precisely. Repeat 3-5 times, and you have a production system people actually want to use.

Based in Durban, South Africa. Currently hiring for full-time roles, freelance projects, and consulting engagements.

My Approach

  • Listen more than talk (especially to users)
  • Make assumptions visible (then test them)
  • Design before coding (always)
  • Test comprehensively (unit + E2E + UAT)
  • Document decisions (not just code)
  • Ship in phases (reduce risk)
  • Measure outcomes (not just features)
